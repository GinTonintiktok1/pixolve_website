Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Pixolve collects, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who We Are

Pixolve is an AI-powered homework solver app developed and operated by Christian Laino, based in Naples, Italy. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at support@pixolve.app.

2. Data We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account data: email address and language preference set during onboarding.
  • Photos & images: images of exercises you upload or snap within the app. These are sent to our AI processing service and are not permanently stored on our servers after processing.
  • In-app purchases: subscription transactions are handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see or store your payment details.

Data collected automatically

  • Usage data: number of daily solves used, subject categories queried, app version, and language setting — used solely to enforce the free-tier limit and improve accuracy.
  • Crash reports: anonymous diagnostics sent when the app crashes, to help us fix bugs. No personal data is included.
  • Device info: OS version, device model, and region — used for compatibility purposes only.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your exercise photos and return step-by-step AI solutions.
  • To enforce the 3 free-solve daily limit on free-tier accounts.
  • To manage your subscription status via App Store / Google Play receipts.
  • To improve AI accuracy and app performance through aggregated, anonymised analytics.
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your exercise photos to train AI models without explicit consent.

4. Third-Party Services

Pixolve relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • OpenAI / AI provider: processes exercise images to generate solutions. Images are transmitted securely and not retained after the response is returned.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: manages all in-app purchase billing and subscription verification.

5. Data Retention

Exercise images are deleted from our servers immediately after your solution is returned. Usage counters (solve count per day) are reset every 24 hours. Account-level data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request full deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@pixolve.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Pixolve is intended for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@pixolve.app or visit our Contact page.
